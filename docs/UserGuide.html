WebPasswordSafe User Guide

Basic Features

Login

Go to the WebPasswordSafe URL in your web-browser. Ask your system administrator how you should authenticate in your environment. Enter your username and password for your environment and click Submit button. If the login fails you will get an error message, if the login is successful WebPasswordSafe main screen will load and show your name as Logged In at the top right.
